Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to Pick2Pay (“Application”), provided by Aleksandr Shirdin (“Service Provider”). The Application is offered free of charge and intended for use as is.

Data Collection and Processing

The Application does not collect or transmit any personal data. All values you enter (e.g., counts, rates, goals) are stored locally on your device in internal storage. The Application does not use Internet access, analytics, or advertising SDKs. Registration is not required to use the Application. Application data may be included in system-level backups (e.g., Google Drive backup) if such backups are enabled on your device. These backups are managed entirely by your device/operating system provider, not by the Service Provider.

Location Information

The Application does not collect precise location data.

Data Sharing

Since no data are collected or transmitted, no data are shared with third parties.

Opt-Out Rights

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the Application or clearing its data in your device settings.

Children

The Application is not directed to children. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU).

Security

All data remain on your device. Please use your device’s built-in security features (such as screen lock or encryption) to protect access.
